01 / EXECUTIVE THESIS

Camp Torch has solved distinctiveness.
It has not fully solved belief.

“Move from spectacle-led curiosity to proof-led conviction before Kickstarter launch.”

The product’s visual hook is unusually strong. But the campaign overuses that hook instead of systematically moving buyers through comprehension, safety, utility, trust and reservation urgency.

WHAT IS WORKING

Memorable product silhouette

Distinct shape, coherent world, emotional territory and a credible social-status hook.

WHAT LIMITS SCALE

Proof arrives too late

Comprehension takes time. Safety is asserted. The offer is fragmented. Creative territories are narrow.

03 / MARKET REALITY

The real enemy is the
practical-but-lifeless campsite.

STRATEGIC ENEMY

The night after wood fires disappear.

Camp Torch is not only competing with fire pits. It competes with every tool people use to make the night usable, warm, social or memorable.

Wood fireRitual + warmthRestricted, smoky, messy
Propane fire pitControl + convenienceBulky and familiar
LED lanternsPractical lightEmotionally flat
String lightsAtmosphereNo warmth or center
Camp TorchFire + light + social gravityMust prove safety and utility

RECOMMENDED POSITIONING

The fire-ban campfire for people who make the night happen.

A modular propane fire and lighting system that converts from a six-foot beacon to a low campfire.

04 / THE CORE MECHANISM

Three fires.

The modular system is the strongest rational differentiator. It should sit beside the hero, not wait mid-page.

01 / BEACON

A six-foot beacon that owns the site.
